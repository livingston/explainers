# Explainers

**Complex documents, explained in plain English.**

Explainers breaks down dense, jargon-heavy documents — laws, regulations, policies — into clear, visual, interactive guides that anyone can understand.

**Live site:** [livingston.github.io/explainers](https://livingston.github.io/explainers/)

---

## Current Explainers

### Income Tax Act 2025 (India)

India's most significant tax reform in 63 years, replacing the Income Tax Act of 1961. This explainer covers all 536 sections, 23 chapters, and 16 schedules of the new Act.

**Source:**

- [Income Tax Act 2025 (Act No. 30 of 2025)](https://www.incometaxindia.gov.in/documents/20117/43006/Income-tax-Act-2025_2026-01-08_05-25-02_9f98ce_en.pdf/6b222800-d461-03aa-eae5-c9e861d087b6?version=2.0&t=1773765951173) — the official 655-page legislative text

## Tech Stack

- **Pure HTML, CSS, vanilla JavaScript** — no frameworks, no build tools
- **CSS custom properties** design system with fluid typography (`clamp()`)
- **Fonts:** Merriweather (headings), Inter (body), JetBrains Mono (citations)
